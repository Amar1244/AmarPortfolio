I’m Amar Chaurasiya, a dedicated Data Analyst, Data Scientist, and AI Developer based in Andheri, Mumbai, with a strong passion for leveraging data to drive innovation. My expertise spans Power BI, Tableau, machine learning, deep learning, NLP, and generative AI, enabling me to create impactful solutions such as an 87% accurate employee retention prediction model, a heart disease risk prediction system, and AI-powered PDF interaction bots using tools like Python, Streamlit, TensorFlow, and Hugging Face. I thrive on transforming complex datasets into actionable insights through advanced analytics and interactive visualizations.

My academic journey includes pursuing Data Science & Artificial Intelligence at the Boston Institute of Analytics, complemented by a B.Sc. in Information Technology from Tolani College of Commerce with a CGPA of 7.08. I’ve honed a robust skill set in Python, SQL, Pandas, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, and more, alongside proficiency in creating dynamic dashboards and optimizing models through techniques like feature engineering and hyperparameter tuning. Projects like a 90.2% accurate term deposit prediction model and a CNN-based digit classifier showcase my ability to blend technical expertise with practical applications.

Driven by a passion for AI automation and problem-solving, I’m committed to pushing the boundaries of data-driven decision-making and AI innovation. My soft skills, including critical thinking, teamwork, and effective communication, allow me to collaborate seamlessly and deliver strategic solutions. I’m excited to connect and explore how I can contribute to your data and AI initiatives with creativity and precision!
